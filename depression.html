Description

A common mood disorder that can have serious impacts on daily life. Waiting any longer than two weeks for help is not advized, since that is the minimum amount of time to be diagnosed. The most serious cases of depression have caused lots of grieving through suicides.

Symptoms

  • persistent sadness
  • loss of interests
  • decreased energy
  • weight loss or gain
  • thoughts of death

Causes

  • brain imbalance
  • poor nutrition
  • physical health
  • drug usage
  • emotional stress
